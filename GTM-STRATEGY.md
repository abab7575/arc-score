## Part 5: GTM Strategy

### Channel Strategy

#### Channel 1: Content + SEO (Months 1-6, ongoing)
**Goal:** Capture demand from brands searching for AI agent readiness info.

**Content pillars:**
1. **State of AI Shopping** — Weekly data from our 276-brand index
   - "X% of top fashion brands can't complete checkout with ChatGPT Operator"
   - "The average e-commerce AI agent readiness score is 34/100"
   - Category leaderboards ("Fashion brands ranked by AI agent readiness")

2. **How-to Guides** — Tactical fixes for specific issues
   - "How to add Schema.org Product markup to Shopify in 10 minutes"
   - "5 robots.txt changes that make your store visible to AI agents"
   - "Why your checkout blocks ChatGPT Operator (and how to fix it)"

3. **Agent Deep Dives** — Explain each AI shopping agent
   - "What is ChatGPT Shopping and how does it find products?"
   - "Amazon Buy For Me: What your store needs to be compatible"
   - "The Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) explained for merchants"

4. **Brand Spotlights** — Public case studies from our data
   - "How Nike scores 67/100 on AI agent readiness"
   - "Glossier vs. Sephora: Which beauty brand is more AI-ready?"

**Publishing cadence:** 3 posts/week (automated from Content Engine + editorial)

**SEO targets:**
- "ai agent ecommerce" / "chatgpt shopping optimization"
- "is my site ready for ai agents" / "ai shopping readiness"
- "[brand name] ai agent score" (branded search from our index)

#### Channel 2: LinkedIn Organic (Months 1-3, daily)
**Goal:** Build authority with e-commerce decision makers.

**Content mix (daily):**
- Data insights from our index (Content Engine auto-generates these)
- Hot takes on AI commerce news
- Brand score spotlights with infographic images
- Educational explainers on AI agent types

**Profile:** Position founder as the "AI agent readiness" expert.

**Engagement strategy:**
- Comment on posts from e-commerce leaders, Shopify execs, AI industry voices
- Share original data nobody else has (we have 276 brands scored)
- Tag brands when we publish their scores (generates engagement + awareness)

#### Channel 3: Strategic Partnerships (Months 2-4)
**Goal:** Get distribution through existing e-commerce ecosystems.

**Target partners:**
1. **Shopify Plus Partners** — Offer them white-label AI readiness audits
2. **E-commerce agencies** — Agency plan with co-branded reports
3. **E-commerce newsletters** — Sponsor or contribute data to Practical Ecommerce, Modern Retail, 2PM
4. **E-commerce conferences** — Speak at Shoptalk, eTail, CommerceNext about AI agent readiness
5. **Shopify App Store** — Listing (even if just linking to our SaaS, not a native app)

#### Channel 4: Product-Led Growth (Months 1-6, ongoing)
**Goal:** Free scores drive word-of-mouth and paid conversion.

**The flywheel:**
1. Brand visits homepage → searches their brand or submits URL
2. Gets free score (public, shareable)
3. Sees gated sections (findings, action plan, agent replays)
4. Subscribes to unlock
5. Shares score with team → team member submits another brand → loop

**Growth levers:**
- Public brand pages are SEO-indexable (every brand = a landing page)
- Scores are shareable ("we got a 42/100 on Robot Shopper 😬")
- Competitive benchmarking creates peer pressure
- Email capture → drip sequence → conversion

#### Channel 5: Outbound (Months 3-6)
**Goal:** Directly reach high-value prospects who haven't found us.

**Approach:**
- Use our own data to find brands scoring poorly (F/D grades)
- Send personalized outreach: "We scanned [brand]. Your AI agent readiness score is 23/100. Here's what's blocking ChatGPT Shopping from finding your products."
- Include 2-3 specific findings as proof
- CTA: "See your full report and action plan"

**Target: 50 outbound emails/week to brands scoring below 40**

**Sample outbound email:**
