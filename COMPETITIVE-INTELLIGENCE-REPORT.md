# ARC Score — Competitive Intelligence & Strategic Report
### March 14, 2026

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**You're building in a greenfield category with no dominant player.**

We found 31 companies across 5 tiers. Your closest direct competitors are 3-4 early-stage startups — none with meaningful traction, none fully self-serve, and none with your "10 AI agent profiles" differentiator. The adjacent space (feed management, SEO auditing) is crowded but they're solving a *different* problem. Enterprise platforms ($5K-$50K/mo) are publishing whitepapers about agentic commerce but haven't built products for it.

**The market is screaming:**
- AI shopping traffic to retail sites grew **4,700% YoY** (Adobe, July 2025)
- AI visitors convert **9x better** than social media traffic (Salesforce)
- **65% of brands are doing NOTHING** to prepare
- McKinsey: AI agents will mediate **$3-5 TRILLION** in global commerce by 2030
- Shopping is now the **3rd most common** GenAI use case (BCG, 9,000 consumers surveyed)

**Your positioning is unique:** "Can AI agents shop on your site?" — nobody else owns this question the way GTmetrix owns speed and SecurityScorecard owns security.

## PART 3: PRICING INTELLIGENCE

### The Market Gap

There's a massive, underserved gap in pricing:

```
FREE ─────── $200/mo ─────── $1,000/mo ─────── $5,000+/mo
 │                │                  │                │
 Free scanners    │    NOBODY HERE   │    Enterprise   │
 Lead-gen tools   │    ← ARC SCORE   │    Sales-led    │
 Point tools      │    OPPORTUNITY   │    6-mo onboard │
```

**Cluster 1 — DIY/Free tools ($0-$50/mo):** Single-purpose, limited scope
**THE GAP ($50-$1,000/mo):** Where ARC Score lives. Mid-market brands ($1M-$50M revenue) that need more than a point tool but won't pay $5K/mo or sit through a sales cycle
**Cluster 2 — Enterprise ($1K-$50K/mo):** Full-service, sales-led, long contracts

### Your Current Pricing vs. Market

| | ARC Score | GTmetrix | Dareboost | DebugBear | SecurityScorecard |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | Public scores | 1 URL, monthly | 5 reports/mo | None | Own score only |
| Entry | $79/mo (Monitor) | $13.50/mo | $22/mo | $99/mo | ~$15K/yr (sales) |
| Mid | $249/mo (Team) | $50/mo | $52/mo | $295/mo | ~$50K/yr |

**Your $79/$249 pricing is correct for this category.** You're not selling commodity speed metrics — you're selling "can AI agents buy from you?" which is novel and high-stakes. But there's room for a higher tier.

### What Customers Hate About Existing Tools

1. **"Book a demo" walls** — Mid-market buyers want self-serve, not a sales call
2. **Per-SKU pricing that explodes** — Feed tools get expensive as catalogs grow
3. **Hidden implementation costs** — Enterprise tools charge $5K-$100K just for setup
4. **Annual lock-in with price creep** — 73% of SaaS raised prices in 2025 (avg +14.2%)
5. **Module fatigue** — Real cost is 2-3x the headline after add-ons
6. **Opaque pricing** — 60% of vendors deliberately mask pricing increases

**Your advantage: transparent, flat pricing, no sales calls, no implementation fee, no lock-in.**

## PART 5: STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### What You Have That Nobody Else Does

1. **10 AI agent profiles with weighted scoring** — No competitor scores against specific agents (ChatGPT vs. Perplexity vs. Google AI Mode). This is your moat.

2. **Actually sending bots** — You don't just check markup. You send real automated browsers to attempt purchases. That's proof, not theory.

3. **Fully self-serve at accessible pricing** — Every serious competitor is either free/lead-gen or enterprise/sales-led. You're the only real SaaS product in the gap.

4. **The "full employee" positioning** — "Put in your URL, get a team member that manages AI commerce for you" is wildly compelling vs. "here's a dashboard."

### Recommended Pricing Structure

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | One-time scan, overall score + 7 categories, top 3 issues, industry average comparison. Email required to save. |
| **Monitor** | $99/mo | 1 brand, weekly automated scans, all 10 agent profiles, full findings + action plan, 90-day history, score alerts (email), 3 competitor comparisons |
| **Team** | $299/mo | 5 brands, daily scans, bot test recordings, unlimited history, Slack/webhook alerts, 10 competitor comparisons, PDF reports |
| **Agency** | $599/mo | 15 brands, white-label reports, client dashboard, priority scanning, API access |

**Why raise from $79/$249:** You're underpriced for the value. SecurityScorecard charges $15K+/yr for analogous outside-in scoring. Your competitors (Aido Lighthouse, Agentic Storefront) aren't publishing prices, suggesting they'll go higher. $99/$299 is still firmly in self-serve approval range, and it positions you as premium — not cheap.

### The 90-Day Steal-Like-an-Artist Roadmap

**Weeks 1-3: Nail the Free Scan**
- URL in → impressive report in under 60 seconds
- Score anxiety: show the number big, show what's failing, show what competitors score
- Email gate to save/export results
- Shareable score badges ("ARC Score: 72/100" embeddable image)
- SEO: make brand scores indexable so brands discover themselves

**Weeks 4-6: Build the Monitoring Engine**
- Automated weekly scans with diff detection
- Score change alerts via email
- Historical trend chart (even 4 weeks of data is compelling)
- "Your score changed" re-engagement emails to free users

**Weeks 7-9: Competitor Benchmarking**
- Pre-scan top brands in each vertical (fashion, electronics, beauty, grocery, etc.)
- Category leaderboards (public — drives SEO and social sharing)
- "Compare" feature: side-by-side agent readiness of 2-3 brands
- "How you stack up" section in every report

**Weeks 10-12: Bot Test Recordings**
- Record actual Puppeteer sessions attempting to browse + buy
- "Watch an AI agent fail on your checkout" — visceral, shareable
- Per-agent recordings (how ChatGPT Shopping sees you vs. how Google AI Mode sees you)
- This is the killer feature nobody else has
